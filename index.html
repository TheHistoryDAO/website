FAQ

How does History DAO raise funds?

We will fundraise through a public, transparent smart contract. This means that the terms of the fundraising campaign will be written into a smart contract on the blockchain, and anyone can view and verify the details of the campaign. The funds raised will be held in the smart contract until the campaign is completed, and the distribution of funds will be automatically executed based on the rules and conditions set forth in the contract. This transparent, decentralized approach will ensure that the fundraising process is fair and transparent, and will enable anyone to participate and support our cause.

If we meet the goal, what will it be used for?

If we meet the fundraising goal, the funds will be used according to the roadmap as follows:

  1. 1. Uncovering the Past:

    Making Web3 History Accessible and Understandable - The funds will be used to develop tools and resources to make the history of Web3 technology more accessible and understandable to a wider audience.

  2. 2. Honoring the Present:

    Creating Everlasting NFT Monuments for Every Builder - The funds will be used to create unique digital artworks that recognize and celebrate the contributions of individuals and organizations involved in the development of Web3 technology. This could include creating a dynamic NFT Monument that showcases the creativity and innovation of Web3 builders and provides recognition for their hard work and dedication.

  3. 3. Shaping the Future:

    Building IRL Monument Installations around the World - The funds will be used to create physical installations of NFT artworks in public spaces, such as parks or museums, to showcase the potential of Web3 technology and its impact on the art world. This could include creating immersive and interactive experiences that allow people to engage with Web3 art and technology in new and exciting ways.

  4. 4. Supporting the Journey:

    Investing in Projects that Advance Web3 History and Culture - The funds will be used to support projects and initiatives that advance the understanding and appreciation of Web3 history and culture. This could include funding projects, supporting community initiatives, and investing in educational programs and events that promote the value and significance of Web3 technology.

    Overall, the funds will be used to support the goals and initiatives outlined in the roadmap, with the aim of advancing the understanding and appreciation of Web3 history and culture.

If I contribute, what would I get?

If you contribute to the campaign, you would receive rewards based on the terms and conditions set forth in the fundraising campaign. These rewards could include:

  1. 1. $HISTORY tokens:

    For every 1 ETH contributed, you would receive 100000 $HISTORY tokens.

  2. 2. A space in the Monument:

    All contributors would receive a space in the Monument of Web3 History and Culture, which is a dynamic NFT that recognizes and celebrates the contributions of individuals and organizations involved in the development of Web3 technology.

  3. 3. Other rewards:

    Depending on the specific terms of the campaign, you may also receive additional rewards, such as DAO airdrop, exclusive access to events or etc.

What happens if the goal is not met?

The fundraising campaign is structured as an all-or-nothing campaign, where the goal must be met in order for the funds to be released, the funds will be returned to the contributors if the goal is not met.
